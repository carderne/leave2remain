leave2remain

Calculate whether you meet the continuous presence requirements in the UK to apply for settlement and naturalization. I AM NOT A LAWYER, I made this for myself following these two documents.

My understanding is that for settlement you can't be outside the UK more than 180 days in any rolling 12-month period. And for naturalization you can't be outside the UK more than 450 days in the 5-year period, nor more than 90 days in the 12 months before applying.

Enter your trips in the fields provided. The chart shows, for each day, how many of the previous 365 days were spent outside the country.
